Tired of switching to an alt account to view someone Instagram stories?
Our service lets you archive and thus revisit an user stories, without leaving a digital footprint. Your username/IP will not be logged nor sent to Instagram servers.

What am I entitled by using the service?

Privacy The service is completely anonymous towards your identity. Your IP/username will not be logged on Instagram servers.
Ease of use You don't need to logout from your account or even use the Instagram app.
Zero Cost The service is completely free of charge and won't bill you as you continue visiting. We do run ads to help keep the service alive.
