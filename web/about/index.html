About

Born as part of Guackamolly Apps free tool suite, Instagram Archiver is the solution you were looking for to preserve your Instagram memories. It allows you to build a timeline of an Instagram profile by downloading and archiving it daily.

Advantages üëç

  1. Hourly Archiving: Instagram Archiver captures your Instagram profile every hour, providing a detailed timeline of your activity.
  2. Story Focus: While we archive your entire profile, our tool is particularly adept at preserving your Instagram Stories, ensuring you never miss a moment.
  3. Free and Easy to Use: Our tool is entirely free and designed with simplicity in mind. With just a few clicks, you can create a comprehensive archive of your Instagram journey.

Downsides üëé

  1. Public profiles only: While we could implement a mechanism to try to connect with a profile user, we won't do it to respect the user privacy.
  2. Limited use: Right now we only download Instagram stories, since these are viewer temporary. On the long run, we will download more data (e.g., reels) to enrich your experience.
  3. Downtimes: Although built to live 24x7, data hoarding is expensive. We will eventually run out of space, so expect downtimes in those times until we find an affordable solution.
